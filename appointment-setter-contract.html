Section 01

Scope of Services

Contractor agrees to perform the following services on behalf of Client:

  • Source, research, and build targeted prospect lists of U.S.-based local service business owners (e.g., dentists, plumbers, HVAC, roofers, locksmiths, chiropractors, auto repair) with annual revenue between $500K–$10M.
  • Execute cold email outreach campaigns designed to generate inbound interest in Client's pay-per-result SEO offer.
  • Book qualified Zoom discovery calls directly onto Client's calendar with verified decision-makers (business owner or primary operator only).
  • Handle all pre-call follow-up, reminders, and confirmations to maximize show rates.
  • Maintain a shared tracking sheet with full contact details, outreach status, call date/time, and confirmation status for every booked appointment.
Section 02

Definition of a Qualified Appointment

For the purposes of this Agreement, a "Qualified Appointment" is defined as a Zoom call that meets ALL of the following criteria:

  • Decision-maker present: The attendee is the business owner or primary operator with authority to purchase marketing services. Gatekeepers, office managers, or employees do not qualify.
  • Attended: The prospect joins the call and remains for a minimum of 10 minutes.
  • U.S.-based: The business operates in the United States.
  • Target niche: The business falls within the agreed local service verticals outlined in Section 1.
  • Not a duplicate: The prospect has not previously been contacted by Client or booked through another Contractor channel within the past 90 days.
No-Show Policy Appointments where the prospect does not attend, attends for fewer than 10 minutes, or cancels prior to the call are not eligible for compensation. Contractor accepts this risk as part of the pay-per-result model.

Section 04

Performance Standards & Expectations

  • Minimum activity: Contractor agrees to contact a minimum of ______ new prospects per week via cold email outreach.
  • Show rate floor: Contractor is expected to maintain a minimum attended show rate of 55% of all booked calls over any rolling 30-day period. Consistent performance below this threshold may result in contract review or termination.
  • Reporting: Contractor will update the shared tracking sheet within 24 hours of each call outcome (attended, no-show, rescheduled, cancelled).
  • Outreach compliance: All outreach must comply with CAN-SPAM Act requirements and applicable U.S. email regulations.
  • Brand representation: Contractor may not misrepresent Client's services, pricing, or guarantees in outreach copy without written approval.
Section 05

Exclusivity & Conflict of Interest

This Agreement is non-exclusive. Contractor may work with other clients, provided that:

  • Contractor does not simultaneously run outreach campaigns for a direct competitor agency offering pay-per-result SEO to the same target verticals without prior written disclosure to Client.
  • Contractor does not share Client's prospect lists, messaging frameworks, or outreach data with any third party.
Section 06

Confidentiality

Contractor agrees to keep confidential all non-public information shared by Client, including but not limited to: prospect lists, client lists, outreach sequences, pricing, offer structure, conversion data, and any business information designated as confidential. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement for a period of 2 years.

Section 07

Intellectual Property

Any outreach sequences, email copy, or prospect lists created specifically for Client under this Agreement are the property of Client upon creation. Contractor retains the right to use anonymized, non-client-specific frameworks and methodologies in future engagements.

Prospect lists built by Contractor using their own tooling and resources remain the property of Contractor unless Client separately compensates for list acquisition.

Section 08

Independent Contractor Status

Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee of Client. Contractor is solely responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and compliance with applicable laws. Client will not withhold income taxes, pay Social Security, or provide employee benefits of any kind.

Section 09

Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 7 days' written notice. Client may terminate immediately and without notice in the event of:

  • Fraudulent appointment booking (fake leads, self-referrals, or inflated show data)
  • Breach of confidentiality
  • Violation of CAN-SPAM or any applicable law
  • Misrepresentation of Client's services to prospects

Upon termination, Client will pay for all verified qualified appointments attended prior to the termination date within 5 business days.

Section 10

Dispute Resolution

In the event of a dispute regarding whether an appointment qualifies for payment, the parties agree to resolve the matter first through good-faith discussion. If unresolved within 7 days, either party may escalate to binding arbitration under the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The prevailing party shall bear their own costs unless the arbitrator rules otherwise.

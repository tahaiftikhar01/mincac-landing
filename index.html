Proof

What happened when we shipped.

Four systems. Four problems that were costing real money. Here's the before and after.

MNC-001 · B2B SaaS · Sales Team Delivered

End-to-End LinkedIn → CRM Pipeline

Problem Data scattered across HeyReach, Clay, and HubSpot. Reps spent hours on manual entry. Missing emails meant prospects were slipping through the cracks entirely.
Built HeyReach webhook → Clay for email enrichment in seconds → n8n pushes clean records into HubSpot automatically. No human effort. No data gaps.
Result
Zero stale lead data. CRM became the single source of truth. Reps follow up faster with better info. Hours of manual work completely eliminated.
MNC-002 · Local Service Business · Lead Gen Delivered

Instagram DM Automation That Runs While You Sleep

Problem Manual hours responding to Instagram DMs. Inconsistent lead quality. No tracking or follow-up system in place.
Built n8n + ManyChat: keyword-triggered DM responses, AI-powered lead qualification, automated capture of names, emails, and interests synced to Google Sheets. Zero coding required.
Result
6–8 hours/week saved. Zero missed leads. Faster responses driving higher conversions. An always-on lead engine working 24/7.
MNC-003 · Enterprise Tech · 6,000+ Employees Delivered

Real-Time Workforce Intelligence & Equity Tracking

Problem Executive team relied on manual, quarterly spreadsheets to track retention, compensation equity, and hiring velocity across 14 departments. Data was 90 days old by the time leadership saw it — impossible to address termination spikes or salary discrepancies proactively.
Built Automated data extraction pipeline pulling raw employee data from siloed HR systems into a centralized warehouse. Deployed a live, interactive dashboard visualizing active headcount, historic termination trends, and demographic salary parity in real time.
Result
40+ hours/month of manual HR reporting eliminated. Executive team now has 24/7 visibility into geographic distribution and performance metrics — spotting flight-risk departments instantly.
MNC-004 · Global B2B Retail · Multi-Region Delivered

Global Sales Velocity & Profit Margins Engine

Problem VP of Sales tracked quotas in the CRM and profitability in a separate ERP. Regional reps hit "gross revenue" targets by heavily discounting — resulting in high-volume but negative-margin deals. Leadership didn't realize they were losing money until the end-of-quarter review.
Built Unified data pipeline feeding a live Tableau intelligence hub. Maps real-time sales vs. dynamic targets across 17 product categories, overlays geographic segment data, and plots a multidimensional scatter matrix to instantly flag high-revenue, negative-profit anomalies.
Result
Identified and paused loss-making discount strategies in US and EMEA markets within week 1. Quarterly reporting prep went from 3 weeks to zero. Sales leadership now assigns quotas on net-profit contribution, not gross volume.
